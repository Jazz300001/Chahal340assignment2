About Us

RocketsRus was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate aerospace engineers and dreamers who envisioned a future where space travel is accessible to everyone. Fueled by their collective expertise and a shared commitment to pushing the boundaries of technology, they embarked on a mission to democratize space exploration. Their goal was to create high-quality, affordable rocketships that could open up the wonders of the cosmos to individuals, researchers, and businesses alike. At RocketsRus, we believe that the final frontier should not be reserved for the few but explored by the many. Our innovative designs and cutting-edge technology ensure that every journey beyond Earth is safe, efficient, and exhilarating. Join us as we make the dream of space travel a reality for all.
